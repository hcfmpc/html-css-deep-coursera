Promoção do dia!!!

Esta semana o Little Lemon te presenteia com a oferta do delicioso Camarão à Marettimo, cozido ao molho de creme de leite e curry, servido com arroz de amêndoas.

Uma das suas principais características é o sabor do curry, que é muito presente e fica ainda mais gostoso na companhia de um camarão fresquinho. O prato pede uma taça de vinho branco!

Welcome to Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a Mediterranean restaurant located in the heart of Illinois. We offer a variety of dishes that will make you feel like you are in the Mediterranean.

Our menu includes a variety of dishes such as hummus, falafel, shawarma, and more. We also offer a variety of drinks such as lemonade, mint tea, and more.

Our restaurant is open every day from 11am to 10pm. We offer dine-in, take-out, and delivery services. We also offer catering services for events such as weddings, birthdays, and more.

Visit us today and experience the taste of the Mediterranean!
